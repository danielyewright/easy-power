## Easy Power

Easy Power is a lightweight Factorio mod that adds the **Arc Reactor**, a low-cost craftable power source that provides configurable, fuel-free electricity for your factory.

Designed for creative play, testing environments, sandbox worlds, and megabase construction, the Arc Reactor allows you to focus on building and optimizing your factory without the complexity of traditional power generation setups.

The power output of each Arc Reactor can be adjusted through the mod settings, giving players complete control over how much energy it contributes to their electrical network.

### Features

- Adds a cheap, craftable Arc Reactor
- Generates power without fuel or maintenance
- Configurable power output through startup settings
- Ideal for creative mode, sandbox worlds, and testing
- Integrates seamlessly with existing electrical networks
- Lightweight and performance-friendly

### Configuration

Easy Power includes a startup setting that allows players and server administrators to define the maximum power output of each Arc Reactor.

Example configurations:

| Output  | Use Case                         |
| :------ | :------------------------------- |
| 10 MW	  | Early-game assistance            |
| 100 MW  | Mid-to-large factory support     |
| 1 GW    | Megabase construction            |
| 10 GW+  | Creative mode and stress testing |

### Intended Use Cases

**Creative Building**
Design and test factory layouts without worrying about power infrastructure.

**Blueprint Development**
Prototype and validate production lines before deploying them in survival play.

**Mod Testing**
Quickly power machines, inserters, and production chains when developing or testing other mods.

**Sandbox & Scenario Maps**
Provide reliable energy for custom scenarios, tutorials, and creative worlds.

### Balance

Easy Power is intentionally designed as a utility and convenience mod rather than a progression-focused gameplay mod. The Arc Reactor exists to remove power generation as a bottleneck, allowing players to concentrate on automation, logistics, factory design, and experimentation.

### Compatibility

The Arc Reactor connects directly to Factorio's standard electrical network and is designed to work alongside most vanilla and modded content.
